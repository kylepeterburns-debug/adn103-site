Week-by-week syllabus with chapters, to‑dos, and download slots

How to add your documents

  1. Upload your PDF/Doc files into the /docs folder (or host them anywhere and paste links).
  2. Edit weeks.json and fill the resources array for each week with titles + URLs.
  3. Re-upload this folder to your host (GitHub Pages, Netlify, or your school server).

Tip: You can also add new sections or pages by duplicating this template.
